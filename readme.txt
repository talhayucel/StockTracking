🎗️StockTracking🎗️
Inventory Tracking Application is ideal for businesses or warehouses. Users can enter data such as barcode number, product information, quantity and price. In addition, the application keeps stock movements up to date by recording them.

------------------------------------
🌟
The interface consists of different sections:

- Available Stocks: 
> A screen where users can view their stocks.

- New Stock Card:
> A form used to add new stock cards.

- Stock Movements: 
> A management interface used to update or delete stocks.
